193 /* 7.18.4.1 Macros for minimum-width integer constants
194 
195  Accoding to Douglas Gwyn <gwyn@arl.mil>:
196  "This spec was changed in ISO/IEC 9899:1999 TC1; in ISO/IEC
197  9899:1999 as initially published, the expansion was required
198  to be an integer constant of precisely matching type, which
199  is impossible to accomplish for the shorter types on most
200  platforms, because C99 provides no standard way to designate
201  an integer constant with width less than that of type int.
202  TC1 changed this to require just an integer constant
203  *expression* with *promoted* type."
204 
205  The trick used here is from Clive D W Feather.
206 */
207 
208 #define INT8_C(val) (INT_LEAST8_MAX-INT_LEAST8_MAX+(val))
209 #define INT16_C(val) (INT_LEAST16_MAX-INT_LEAST16_MAX+(val))
210 #define INT32_C(val) (INT_LEAST32_MAX-INT_LEAST32_MAX+(val))
211 #define INT64_C(val) (INT_LEAST64_MAX-INT_LEAST64_MAX+(val))
